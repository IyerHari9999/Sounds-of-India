ABOUT


Sounds of India (SoI) is a page exclusively created by youngsters for promoting Indian Music among the youth by launching various events and organizing programmes and concerts through their Facebook page and YouTube Channel.

The Page has organized many events like Bhajanotsavam in the Navaratri Season and various other Carnatic concerts. With the blessings and support of all currently the Dasavani Series (Compositions by Saints of India) is running with great success.
